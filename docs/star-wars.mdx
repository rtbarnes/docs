## Overview

Star Wars is an epic space-opera franchise created by George Lucas. It began with the 1977 film *Star Wars* (later subtitled *Episode IV: A New Hope*) and has since grown into a multi-billion dollar media franchise that includes films, television series, novels, comics, and video games.

The saga is set "a long time ago in a galaxy far, far away" and follows the conflict between the light and dark sides of the Force.

## The Skywalker Saga

The main film series consists of three trilogies that follow the Skywalker family across generations.

## The Force

The Force is a mystical energy field that binds the galaxy together. Those sensitive to the Force can train to use it, falling into two main traditions:

- **The Jedi** use the light side, emphasizing peace, knowledge, and defense.
- **The Sith** draw on the dark side, channeling passion, anger, and the pursuit of power.
