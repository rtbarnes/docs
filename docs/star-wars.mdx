## Overview

Star Wars is an epic space-opera multimedia franchise created by George Lucas. The original film, released in 1977, became a worldwide phenomenon and launched one of the most influential and recognizable franchises in entertainment history.

## The Saga

The Skywalker Saga spans nine mainline films divided into three trilogies, exploring the conflict between the Jedi and the Sith across multiple generations.

## The Force

The Force is a mystical energy field that binds the galaxy together. Force-sensitive individuals can tap into it to perform extraordinary feats.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="The Light Side" icon="sun">
    Practiced by the Jedi, the light side emphasizes peace, knowledge, serenity, and selflessness. Jedi use the Force to defend and preserve life.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="The Dark Side" icon="moon">
    Practiced by the Sith, the dark side draws power from passion, anger, and hatred. It offers quick power but corrupts those who use it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
