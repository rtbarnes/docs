## Overview

Star Wars is an epic space-opera franchise created by [George Lucas](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/George_Lucas). It began with the 1977 film *Star Wars* (later subtitled *A New Hope*) and has since expanded into one of the largest media franchises in history, spanning films, television series, books, comics, and games.

## The saga

The core Star Wars saga is structured into three trilogies, with additional standalone films and series filling in the timeline.

## The Force

The Force is a metaphysical, binding, and ubiquitous power that holds enormous importance for both the Jedi and Sith. It has two aspects:

- **The light side**: Embraced by the Jedi, focused on peace, knowledge, and selflessness.
- **The dark side**: Wielded by the Sith, fueled by passion, anger, and a hunger for power.

<Note>
  "May the Force be with you." — A common farewell across the galaxy.
</Note>

## Iconic factions

| Faction          | Description                                                                 |
| ---------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Jedi Order       | Guardians of peace and justice who wield the light side of the Force.       |
| Sith             | Ancient order seeking power through the dark side of the Force.             |
| Galactic Empire  | Authoritarian regime led by Emperor Palpatine.                              |
| Rebel Alliance   | Coalition fighting to restore the Galactic Republic.                        |
| First Order      | Successor to the Empire, opposed by the Resistance.                         |

## Beyond the films

The Star Wars universe extends far beyond the theatrical releases:

- **Television**: *The Mandalorian*, *Andor*, *Ahsoka*, *The Clone Wars*, and *Rebels*.
- **Books and comics**: Hundreds of novels and comic series expanding the lore.
- **Games**: Titles such as *Knights of the Old Republic*, *Jedi: Fallen Order*, and *Battlefront*.
