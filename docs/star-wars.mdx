Star Wars is a space opera franchise created by George Lucas. Since the release of the original film in 1977, it has expanded into films, television series, novels, comics, and games.

## The saga

The core films are organized into three trilogies that follow the rise, fall, and redemption of the Skywalker family.

- **Original trilogy** (1977–1983): *A New Hope*, *The Empire Strikes Back*, *Return of the Jedi*.
- **Prequel trilogy** (1999–2005): *The Phantom Menace*, *Attack of the Clones*, *Revenge of the Sith*.
- **Sequel trilogy** (2015–2019): *The Force Awakens*, *The Last Jedi*, *The Rise of Skywalker*.

## The Force

The Force is an energy field that binds the galaxy together. Practitioners fall broadly into two traditions:

- **Jedi** draw on the light side, valuing discipline, peace, and selflessness.
- **Sith** draw on the dark side, fueled by passion, ambition, and power.

## Notable characters

- **Luke Skywalker** — farm boy turned Jedi who helps overthrow the Empire.
- **Leia Organa** — princess, senator, and leader of the Rebellion.
- **Han Solo** — smuggler turned Rebel hero, captain of the *Millennium Falcon*.
- **Darth Vader** — fallen Jedi and enforcer of the Galactic Empire.
- **Obi-Wan Kenobi** — Jedi Master who trains both Anakin and Luke Skywalker.

## Beyond the films

The franchise extends well past the main saga, including live-action series such as *The Mandalorian* and *Andor*, animated series like *The Clone Wars* and *Rebels*, and a deep catalog of novels, comics, and video games.
