Star Wars is a multimedia franchise created by George Lucas, beginning with the 1977 film *Star Wars: Episode IV – A New Hope*. It has grown into one of the most influential science fiction sagas in history, spanning films, television series, novels, comics, and games.

## The saga

The story follows the timeless conflict between the light and dark sides of the Force, a mystical energy that binds the galaxy together. Across generations, Jedi and Sith battle for the fate of the galaxy.

### The Skywalker Saga

The nine mainline films are divided into three trilogies:

- **Original trilogy** (1977–1983): *A New Hope*, *The Empire Strikes Back*, *Return of the Jedi*
- **Prequel trilogy** (1999–2005): *The Phantom Menace*, *Attack of the Clones*, *Revenge of the Sith*
- **Sequel trilogy** (2015–2019): *The Force Awakens*, *The Last Jedi*, *The Rise of Skywalker*

## Iconic characters

- **Luke Skywalker** — A farm boy from Tatooine who becomes a legendary Jedi Knight.
- **Darth Vader** — The fallen Jedi Anakin Skywalker, turned Sith Lord.
- **Princess Leia** — A leader of the Rebel Alliance and twin sister to Luke.
- **Han Solo** — A smuggler turned hero, captain of the *Millennium Falcon*.
- **Yoda** — The wise Grand Master of the Jedi Order.
- **Obi-Wan Kenobi** — A Jedi Master who trained both Anakin and Luke.

## The Force

> "The Force will be with you. Always." — Obi-Wan Kenobi

The Force is the spiritual heart of the saga, granting practitioners abilities such as telekinesis, foresight, and mind influence. Its two sides — light and dark — represent the central moral conflict of the universe.

## Beyond the films

The franchise also includes acclaimed series like *The Mandalorian*, *Andor*, and *Ahsoka*, as well as animated shows such as *The Clone Wars* and *Rebels*, expanding the galaxy far, far away into new eras and stories.
