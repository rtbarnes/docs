## The origami unicorn

In the 1982 film *Blade Runner*, the character Gaff (played by Edward James Olmos) leaves small origami figures throughout the movie. The final figure — a silver origami unicorn left outside Deckard's apartment — is one of cinema's most debated clues.

Earlier in the film, Deckard daydreams about a unicorn. The fact that Gaff knows about this private dream has led many viewers (and director Ridley Scott himself) to conclude that Deckard's memories aren't his own, implying he is a replicant.

Edward James Olmos folded the origami props himself on set, drawing on his real-life hobby of paper folding.
