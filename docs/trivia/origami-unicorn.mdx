## Trivia

In *Blade Runner* (1982), Gaff (Edward James Olmos) leaves a small origami unicorn outside Deckard's apartment in the film's final scene. Earlier, Deckard dreams of a unicorn running through a forest — a sequence added to Ridley Scott's later cuts of the film.

The implication is that Gaff knows the contents of Deckard's private dreams, suggesting that Deckard himself may be a replicant whose memories were implanted. Harrison Ford has publicly disagreed with this interpretation, while Ridley Scott has confirmed it.

The unicorn was folded on set by Olmos, who took up origami specifically for the role and improvised most of the paper figures that appear throughout the film.
