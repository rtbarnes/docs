---
title: "Blade Runner trivia: the origami unicorn"
description: "A small piece of Blade Runner lore about Gaff's origami unicorn."
---

In Ridley Scott's *Blade Runner* (1982), Gaff (played by Edward James Olmos) leaves a small origami unicorn outside Deckard's apartment in the film's final scene.

Earlier, Deckard has a daydream of a unicorn running through a forest. Because Gaff somehow knows about this private vision, the unicorn is widely read as evidence that Deckard's memories — and Deckard himself — may be implanted, suggesting he is a replicant.

Ridley Scott has confirmed this reading in multiple interviews, while Harrison Ford has pushed back against it for decades — a disagreement that has fueled fan debate ever since.
