## Did you know?

Tom Bombadil is one of the only beings in Middle-earth completely unaffected by the One Ring. When Frodo hands the Ring to him in *The Fellowship of the Ring*, Tom puts it on and does not vanish — and when he hands it back, the Ring fails to make Frodo invisible to him.

Tolkien himself described Tom as "an enigma" and deliberately left his origins unexplained. At the Council of Elrond, the wise specifically reject the idea of giving the Ring to Tom for safekeeping — not because he would be corrupted, but because he would likely forget about it or simply lose interest and throw it away.
